Why Khoon Naama Exists

Built From
Personal Experience

When I was diagnosed with anemia, I received a lab report full of numbers — Hemoglobin, MCH, MCHC, MCV — and had no idea what any of it meant. My doctor used medical terms. The internet gave me anxiety. Nobody explained it in simple language.

So I built Khoon Naama — an AI tool that reads your CBC report and explains it the way a knowledgeable friend would. Not a doctor. A friend.
