Why I Built This

"I was diagnosed with anemia and couldn't find a simple tool to understand my results."

Most medical tools are confusing, cold, and made for doctors — not for people like us. I wanted something warm, simple, and honest. Something that tells you not just what your numbers are, but what they mean — and what to eat for lunch.

Khoon Naama is that tool. Built with real clinical data, machine learning, and a lot of personal experience.
